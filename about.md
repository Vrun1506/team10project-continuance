# About

- **Pick-up-and-play:** Learn in a minute, master in a few runs.
- **Replayable:** Beat your best times and try alternate routes.
- **Competition:** Go up against your friends one after the other to see who can top the leaderboard
- **Fun events!** Can you find them all?


Escape From Uni is a Java-based game created as part of the Engineering 1 Module at the University of York.
It's a map-based game based on university life as a student at York and be prepared to discover a few similarities with real life!
Can you complete the game and top our leaderboard? 
Can you get all of the achievements? 

Play the game and find out for yourself!
